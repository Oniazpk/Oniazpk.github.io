About ONIAZ

Company Profile

Founded in 2025

ONIAZ is a purpose-driven startup built on a foundation of deep experience and bold innovation. Our journey is more than just about launching another tech company — it's about combining decades of expertise with a shared desire to create smart, human-centered solutions that matter.

Behind ONIAZ is a close-knit team of four visionary professionals, each bringing over 18 years of industry experience to the table. From tech architecture and user experience design to business development, project management, and strategic marketing, we've honed our skills through years of working with startups, enterprises, and global brands.

Now, we've joined forces under a single mission: to craft tech that's not just functional but transformational. ONIAZ is the intersection of insight and imagination — a space where our collective knowledge is poured into building applications that are clean, intuitive, and genuinely helpful.
